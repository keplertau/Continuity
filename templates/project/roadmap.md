# Roadmap

This file is forward-looking only.

The roadmap carries committed work and current priorities. **Completed items are removed entirely** — their record lives in session entities and decision entities. The roadmap should stay small enough to be useful at session start.

If you find yourself wanting to keep a record of what has been done, that is the job of `sessions/` and `decisions/`, not this file.

## Speculative / Backlog

If you maintain longer-horizon ideas separately (the public Continuity SPEC's pattern), keep them out of this file. They belong in `backlog.md` at the project root or in `archive/backlog.md`. Items move from backlog to roadmap when they become the next thing to do.

If your project is small enough that a separate backlog is overhead, a "Speculative" section here is fine. Keep it clearly separated from committed work.

## What does **not** go here

- Completed work (that's in `sessions/` and `decisions/`)
- Operating stances or rules (those are in `decisions/`)
- Open questions or unresolved tensions (those are in `open-threads.md`)
- Active processes or cadences (those are in `processes/`)
- Generative ideas without a place yet (those are in `fragments/` if the folder exists)
