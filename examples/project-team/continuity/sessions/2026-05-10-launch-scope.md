## What Happened

The team reviewed whether the v1.0 launch should emphasize breadth of feature examples or reliability of the first-user path.

The strongest feature-breadth case was that examples demonstrate usefulness better than abstract concepts. The counterargument held: early testers were not failing because they lacked imagination about use cases; they were failing before they reached one successful run.

The session produced the docs-first launch decision. Quickstart, Concepts, and Maintainer Guide became the launch gate.
